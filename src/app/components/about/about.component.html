About Us

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in faith, and serve our community with compassion, humility, and love.

Our Vision

To be a Christ‑centered church where people grow spiritually, build strong relationships, and impact the world around them.

Our History

ACK St James Koromosho has been a pillar of faith in our community for years, bringing people together through worship, fellowship, and service.

Our Core Values

Love

We show Christ’s love through compassion, kindness, and service.

Community

We grow together in fellowship, unity, and mutual support.

Faith

Service
